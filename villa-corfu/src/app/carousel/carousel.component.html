Welcome to Villa Inva Korfu

A 10-minute walk from Kavos Beach in Kavos, Villa INVA Korfu features accommodations with access to a hot tub, wellness packages, and beauty services. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk, and full-day security, along with free Wifi throughout the property. There's free private parking, and the property provides a paid airport shuttle service.

Providing a terrace and mountain views, the villa includes 3 bedrooms, a living room, satellite flat-screen TV, an equipped kitchen, and 3 bathrooms with a walk-in shower and a bidet. A private entrance leads guests into the villa, where they can enjoy some wine or champagne and fruit. The villa offers bed linen, towels, and laundry service.

Guests at the villa can enjoy a continental or a full English/Irish breakfast. There is a coffee shop, and packed lunches are also available.

Yoga classes and fitness classes are organized at the fitness room on site. For guests with children, Villa INVA Korfu offers an indoor play area, outdoor play equipment, and a baby safety gate. Skiing and cycling can be enjoyed nearby, while a bicycle rental service and a private beach area are also available on-site.

Arkoudilas Beach is 1.3 miles from the accommodation, while Achilleion Palace is 23 miles away. Corfu International Airport is 30 miles from the property.
